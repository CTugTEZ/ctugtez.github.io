I am a candidate of Ph. D. at the Ataturk University at Erzurum. I received my M. Sc. at Ankara University Astronomy and Space Sciences in January 2016.

I am working as a Specialist Astronomer at Dogu Anadolu Gozlemevi - DAG - project (Eastern Anatolia Observatory). DAG is a project of establishing 4 meter, NIR telescope with active and Adaptive Optics at 3170 meter altitude in Erzurum, Turkey. I am interested with telescope, observatory and instrumentation technologies. Beside I am study about data analysis and observations of exoplanets.

You can find my works and papers at this personel website.
